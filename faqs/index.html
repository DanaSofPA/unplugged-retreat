Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

Lake Geneva is in Southeastern Wisconsin, approximately 10 miles from the Illinois state line. If you drive here, we have plenty of free parking. If you fly into Chicago's O'Hare Airport, we are a 90-minute drive. Or if you fly into Milwaukee's General Mitchell International Aiport, we are a 45-minute drive. For an additional fee, we can transport your group to/from the Milwaukee airport in our 12-passenger van.

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

If your team regularly works together at your home site, we'll design a retreat that helps build communication and collaboration. If your team is one that primarily works remotely, we'll make sure that the retreat has plenty of opportunities for team members to get to know each other better. Regardless of your work set-up, we'll custom-design a retreat that meets the needs of your group.

What size teams can you accommodate?

We can accommodate teams of 4 - 40.

Is there really no wifi available?

Your Unplugged retreat is truly unplugged. But if an emergency requires you to access wifi, we can help you with that in our Retreat Office.
